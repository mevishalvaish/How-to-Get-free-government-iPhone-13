Understanding Government Assistance Programs

Two main FCC programs make these offers possible:

  • Lifeline Support: A federal program that lowers the monthly cost of phone or internet service. It has been ensuring Americans stay connected since 1985.
  • Affordable Connectivity Program (ACP): A benefit program that helps ensure households can afford the broadband they need for work, school, healthcare, and more.

While the ACP has faced funding changes, many providers like AirTalk Wireless, Cintex Wireless, and NewPhone Wireless continue to offer free smartphones, including iPhones, to qualifying customers who enroll in their specific plans.

Eligibility Checklist

To qualify for a free Government iPhone 13, you typically need to participate in a government assistance program or meet income criteria. Check if you fall into one of these categories:

  • Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP/Food Stamps)
  • Medicaid / Medi-Cal
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance (Section 8)
  • Veterans Pension and Survivors Benefit
  • Household income at or below 135% of the Federal Poverty Guidelines
